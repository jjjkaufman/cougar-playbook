Egor Demin is sure to be a first-round NBA draft pick. He was BYU's highest rated recruit in the history of the men's basketball program, and he is sure to go on to big things. Some sources have Demin getting drafted in the late lottery, while others have him getting drafted just outside the lottery. Some sources even predict the Egor Demin will be drafted to the Utah Jazz with the 18th overall pick. Some other sources predict Demin will go anywhere between 12th overall and 17th overall in the first round. Depending on where Demin gets drafted, the value of potential trades increases.
